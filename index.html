Is Z-Image free to use?

You can start using Z-Image for free, allowing you to explore its features at no cost.

How accurate is Z-Image in following prompts?

Z-Image demonstrates strong prompt understanding and follows detailed instructions well, making it suitable for structured and creative image tasks.

What type of images can Z-Image create?

Z-Image can generate a wide range of images including portraits, product visuals, artistic renders, and creative concepts with realistic detail.
